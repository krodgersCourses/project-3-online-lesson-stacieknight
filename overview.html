What to do with all of that tech?

Welcome! Today we will explore a range of technology tools designed to support effective teaching and enrich student learning experiences.

Home | Resources | Assessment

Learning Objectives

  • Identify various technology tools that can enhance teaching and learning.
  • Understand how to integrate these tools into lesson plans aligning to the Idaho Content Standards.
  • Evaluate the effectiveness of different technology tools in educational settings.

    • Lesson Activity

    Take a moment to reflect on the technology tools you currently use in your classroom. Take a look at the list of tools provided in the resources section. Consider how these tools can be integrated into your lesson plans to enhance student engagement and learning outcomes. Take a look at the Idaho Content Standards and find which standard you'd like to focus on. Write a lesson plan that incorporates at least one technology tool. Share your lesson plan with a peer for feedback.
